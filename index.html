John is a freelance composer based in the beautiful countryside of West Berkshire in England. He has experience working in video games, audio drama, theatre, writing to picture and audio branding, and draws on his theatrical and storytelling background to bring a narrative awareness to his projects.

John holds a Master's Degree in Professional Media Composition from the University of Chichester in the UK. He has two dogs, loves board games, and relaxes by building Lego models.
